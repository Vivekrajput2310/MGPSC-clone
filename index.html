The MP SET exam took place on December 15, 2024, and the Provisional Answer Key was released on December 24, 2024, covering 12 different subjects. The MP SET is a state-level examination organized by the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) to recruit Assistant Professors for colleges and universities across Madhya Pradesh. The Madhya Pradesh (MP) SET 2024 exam consists of two papers: Paper 1 and Paper 2. Paper 1 includes 50 questions to be completed in 60 minutes, whereas Paper 2 consists of 100 questions with a time limit of 120 minutes.
